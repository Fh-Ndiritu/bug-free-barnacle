# Cash is Clear Maximizer

Browse and filter cash flow & profit optimization strategies from your Excel data file.

**Live site:** https://fh-ndiritu.github.io/bug-free-barnacle/

---

## How to use

### Step 1 — Convert your Excel file

1. Go to the **[Admin Converter Tool](https://fh-ndiritu.github.io/bug-free-barnacle/admin/)**
2. Upload your `Cash is Clear Maximizer` `.xlsx` file
3. Click **Download data.json** to save the converted file to your computer

### Step 2 — Load the data into the app

1. Go to the **[main app](https://fh-ndiritu.github.io/bug-free-barnacle/)**
2. Click **Upload JSON** and select the `data.json` file you just downloaded
3. The app will load your strategies — use the filters and search to explore them
